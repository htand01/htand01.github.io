Heidi Tandiono

Illustrator & Data Analyst

Hi, I’m Heidi—a data analyst and artist based in the Greater Seattle Area. Storytelling has been part of my life since childhood, whether through numbers, visuals, or creative expression. I love exploring patterns in data and turning complex information into insights that anyone can understand. At the same time, my artistic practice often begins in my sketchbook, where I capture memories, places I’ve visited, and moments that spark inspiration. Through both analytics and artwork, I hope to share a little of my world with you.
